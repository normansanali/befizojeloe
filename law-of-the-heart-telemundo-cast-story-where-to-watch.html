Law of The Heart Telemundo: Cast, Story, Where To Watch

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, July 3, 2024

Law of the heart (La Ley del Corazón) is a Colombia telenovela released on 28 November 2018. It is also known as Heart’s Decree. The telenovela was created by Mónica Agudelo Tenorio. If you are searching on Law of the heart Telemundo, then read on as we give you the full cast, story and where to watch it.

When Was La Ley del Corazón (Heart’s Decree) Released?

La Ley del Corazón was released on 28 November 2018. The show was shot in Colombia. Heart’s Decree ended on April 22 2019.

Where To Watch La Ley del Corazón (Heart’s Decree)

The show was available on RCN Televisión in Colombia but ended on April 22 2019. Those hoping to watch online can visit this website.

La Ley del Corazón (Heart’s Decree) Story Summary

The series takes place in a successful law firm specializing in family law, dedicated to cases of separation and in general to family conflicts and couples. Pablo Domínguez, a partner lawyer at the firm Cabal-Ortega-Domínguez and associates, is going through a difficult situation in his life when separating from his wife Jimena.

Suddenly, the lawyer meets his colleague Julia Escallón, who is about to marry Camilo Borrero, but fate will give an unexpected turn in the life of the woman on the day of their marriage and by things of the Life, will end up very close to Pablo and facing him in the courts.
