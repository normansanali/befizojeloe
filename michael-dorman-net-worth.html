Michael Dorman Net Worth

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, July 19, 2024

Michael Dorman

$5 million

Michael Dorman is a New Zealand actor whose career started in the 2000s. He debuted in the film Spudmonkey. He next starred as Christian Hayden in the Australian series The Secret Life of Us, which he played from 2000 to 2005. After this, he had performances in the films 50 Ways of Saying Fabulous, Suburban Mayhem, and West. He went on to star in the movies Acolytes, Prime Mover, Triangle, Daybreakers, and Needle. In 2011, he made his international debut in the movie Killer Elite. He next appeared in the film Sleeping Beauty. From 2013 to 2015, he starred as Tom Wilcox in the Aussie show Wonderland. He next had performances in the films The Water Diviner, Goldstone, and Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Men Tell No Tales. Dorman recently starred in the 2020 film The Invisible Man.
