Kirk Herbstreit Biography

Kirk Herbstreit is an American Sports Analyst, Writer, Producer, and Former Football Player, who was born and brought up in Centerville, Ohio, United States of America. He is an analyst for ESPN’s College GameDay, a television program covering college football, and as a color commentator on college football games on ESPN and ABC.

Kirk has received five Sports Emmy Awards including Three Outstanding Studio Analyst in 2010, 2011, and 2019, and Two Outstanding Event Analyst in 2018 and 2020. He played for Ohio State University from 1989 to 1993, as a quarterback. He was nominated for a 1997 Sports Emmy Award as television’s top studio analyst.
