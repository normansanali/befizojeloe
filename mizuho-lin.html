Mizuho Lin is a Brazilian Metal Singer. Mizuho Lin is a lead singer for the metal band “Semblant“. Likewise, she has performed as a soprano with a band called Lords of Aesir.

Birth Facts

Mizuho Lin was born on May 26, 1990, in Brazil. She is 33 years old and is under the birth sign, Gemini. She holds Brazilian nationality. Likewise, she hails from Foz do Iguaçu, Paraná, Brazil. Her ethnicity is Chinese-Brazilian.

Career and Professional Life

Mizuho is a professional vocalist. She has been serving as the lead vocalist for the Semblant band since the year 2010. Likewise, other members of the bands are Sergio Mazul who is also a Vocalist, Sol Perez, and Juliano Ribeiro who are Guitarists, J. Augusto who is a Keyboard player, and Thor Sikora, a Drummer. Moreover, she released an EP titled “Behind the Mask” in the year 2011. She also released a single titled “Throw Back to Hell” in the year 2012. Later in the year 2014, she released an album titled “Lunar Manifesto”.

She has performed in two music videos, “Dark of the Day” and “What Lies Ahead”. She performed the videos along with Semblant. And, additionally, Lin has also uploaded several cover videos on her YouTube channel. She owns a Youtube channel under the name “Mizuho Lin”. She launched the channel on April 8, 2006, and has accumulated altogether 548,472 views until now.

Furthermore, she has even collaborated with renowned musicians Moonspell and Warrel Dane. And, besides being a singer, she is also a businesswoman. She runs a bubble tea shop in Brazil which she founded in the year 2016. Moreover, Lin also works as a vocal coach as well. She was a guest vocalist for the band Ankhy in the year 2013.
