Snowbird Brown wiki-bio, age , net worth, family, sibling, facts.

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

Snowbird Brown is American reality TV star. She is known as the cast of Discovery TV series “Alaskan Bush People” alongside her family members. The show captures the lifestyle of Brown family living in the Copper River Valley, Alaska, where the temperature can fall as low as minus 60 degrees. According to discovery channel, they live a day to day life away from civilization which often leaves them without seeing an outsider six to nine months each year. In this article, we are covering particular Brown, Snowbird Brown who is the sixth child of Billy Brown and Amy Brown.

Snowbird Brown was born on November 18, 1994, in Alaska. She was raised in deep bushes of Alaska alongside her five brothers and a younger sister. While growing up she developed a strong love for wild nature and outdoor activities. She is a keen animal lover, at one point she had dozens of pet including frogs, five Red-Eared Slider Turtles, many cats, dogs and even a squirrel. She may have soft spot for natural beauty but when it comes to survival she is a tough girl. She learned fishing and hunting from a young age and plays a part providing food for the family.

Snowbird Brown wiki/bio Facts

Snowbird Brown parents

Her parents Billy and Ami Brown led a life in the wilderness of Alaska for decades now. Surprisingly they are not Alaskan native as they hailed from a small town in North Texas. The couple only moved after few years of marriage. Billy even has published autobiography “One Wave at a Time” which focuses on his years of struggles to survive and raise a family, in Alaska.

Snowbird Brown Siblings

She has six siblings, five elder brothers, and a younger sister. Her brothers are Matthew Jeremiah Brown (Matt), Joshua Bam Bam Brown( Bam Bam), Solomon Isaiah Freedom “Bear” Brown(Bear), Gabriel Starbuck Brown (Gabe), Noah Darkcloud Brown (Noah) and a sister is Merry Christmas Katherine Raindrop “Rainy” Brown (Rain).

Snowbird Brown Net Worth

Snowbird Brown has amassed a net worth of $200,000 as of 2024. She earns most of her income from appearing in the popular series Alaskan Bush People on the Discovery Channel. She has been part of the show since its debut in 2014 and has appeared in over 100 episodes.
