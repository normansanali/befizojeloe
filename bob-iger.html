Bob Iger is an American Businessman, Chairman of the board, and the former CEO of The Walt Disney Company. Bob Iger was one of the important people in the reinvigoration of Walt Disney Animation Studios.

Early Life

He was born into the world on February 10, 1951, in New York City, New York, United States of America. As of 2024, he is 73 years old. His father’s name is Arthur L. Iger and his mother’s name is Mimi Tunick.

There is no data on his siblings. He follows the Christian religion and belongs to the White Caucasian ethnicity. His Zodiac sign is Aquarius.

As for his education, he studied at the Fulton Avenue School and later graduated from the Oceanside High School in 1969. Moreover, he also graduated with a degree in Television and Radio with a Bachelor of Science from Ithaca College.

Professional Life

Then in 1988, he was named the senior program executive for the Calgary Winter Olympics. During the weather-district delay events, he focused on interesting stories to broadcast the schedule.

Later one year he was named ABC Entertainment head. Then in 1993, he was vice president of ABC and then in 1994, he was the president of ABC corporate parents.
