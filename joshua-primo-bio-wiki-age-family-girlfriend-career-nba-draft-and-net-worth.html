Joshua Primo Career

High school

He played basketball for Huntington Prep School in Huntington, West Virginia, where he was teammates with JT Thor. Primo transferred to Royal Crown Academic School in Scarborough, Toronto. He was reclassified to the 2020 class and graduated early. Considered a five-star recruit by 247Sports, Primo committed to playing college basketball for Alabama, choosing the Crimson Tide over Creighton.

College career

Primo was the youngest player in college basketball in his freshman season. At the time he averaged 8.1 points and 3.4 rebounds per game and was named to the Southeastern Conference  (SEC) All-Freshman Team. He missed three games with a left medial collateral ligament sprain. He declared for the 2021 NBA draft while maintaining his college eligibility on April 21, 2021.

Primo announced he was going to remain in the draft due to a strong performance at the NBA combine.

 Professional

He was selected with the 12th pick in the 2021 NBA draft by the San Antonio Spurs.
