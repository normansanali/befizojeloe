American rapper, actor, and composer Bizzy Bone has a net worth of $2 million in 2022.

Bizzy Bone: Career

Bizzy Bone started his solo music career in 1998, releasing the Heaven’z Movie album, which garnered critical acclaim and was gold-certified by the RIAA.

Subsequently, he released the album ‘The Gift’ in 2001, and it peaked at number 2 on the Billboard Independent Albums chart.

He also released ‘Alpha and Omega,’ ‘The Story,’ ‘Midwest Cowboy,’ etc. to strongly positive reviews. 

Then, he reunited with Layzie Bone in 2008 to create Bone Brothers, releasing two albums.

He released his 10th studio album, ‘Carbon Monoxide,’ in 2019, which entered the iTunes Chart at #3.

In 2020, he released his follow-up album named ‘The Mantra‘ on the occasion of his 44th birthday, and it debuted at number 4 on the iTunes chart.
