Rapper: What Is Nick Cannon Religion, Is He Muslim? Children Wife &amp; Net Worth Explored

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

What is Nick Cannon Religion? As he prepares for season six of The Masked Singer, some fans wonder why he wears a turban on the weekly singing competition show, asking questions about his religion.

Nick Cannon made headlines lately when his fourth baby mother gave birth to his seventh child.

When it comes to turban style, Nick has a keen eye. Take, for example, his appearances on Masked Singer. He made a statement in Season 1 by wearing a crimson, sparkling turban with a patchy, Yves Klein blue, blood-red coloured pattern.

What Is Nick Cannon Religion? Is He Muslim?

Nick Cannon is not a Muslim because he is a committed Christian. He spoke about a “Christian relationship” in which he was celibate and attended “ministry-couple classes” in one interview.

However, people speculate that he might be a Muslim because he wore a turban. 

In an interview with The Ellen DeGeneres Show, Cannon referred to his turban as his “crown” and said he wears it to promote “cultural understanding.”

Cannon says he’s been studying turbans for “a long time” and wears them to encourage others to “accept diversity.”

He described his extensive interest in many religions and cultures and his desire to foster a more progressive, open-minded, and liberal mindset.

“If you could all just accept it and just everybody love each other, no matter what background you’re from, no matter what religion or culture you’re from,” he remarked.

Nick Cannon Children & Wife Explored 

Nick Cannon has twins Moroccan and Monroe, ten, with Mariah Carey, whom he divorced in 2012.

With Brittany Bell, the TV personality has a five-year-old son, Golden, and an eight-month-old daughter, Powerful.

In June, he gave birth to twins Zillion and Zion with Abby De La Rosa and a son Zen with Alyssa Scott.

Cannon previously told Charlamagne Tha God about the backlash he’s been receiving since welcoming his fourth child with his third baby mama in less than a year.

While speaking on the Breakfast Club, Cannon replied, “Why do people question it?” when asked about having numerous children with various women.

Cannon poked fun of his increasing brood on Kevin Hart’s talk show Hart to Heart when Hart inquired who he is “as a dad.”

The presenter of The Masked Singer quipped that he “doesn’t know” how many children he had, estimating “eight or nine.”

“I’m a father to a whole lot of mother***ers,” Cannon responded to Hart, laughing.

When asked how many children he has, Hart playfully said, “I don’t know.”

Nick Cannon Net Worth Revealed 

Cannon’s net worth is estimated to be $30 million as of 2021.

He doesn’t seem to stop amassing wealth and fame as his career progresses.

He is a multi-talented actor who has been a part of the entertainment industry since he was a teenager. Cannon is most known for hosting the television show “America’s Got Talent” and being an actor and singer.
