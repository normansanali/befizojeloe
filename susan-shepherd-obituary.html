Notification of Burial Services

Upcoming Burial Service for Susan L. Shepherd

We would like to inform you of the upcoming burial service to honor and celebrate the life of Susan L. Shepherd. This service, scheduled to take place on March 22, 2023, at the serene Willoughby Memorial Gardens, will be a solemn occasion to pay tribute to Susan’s remarkable journey.

Susan’s life was a tapestry woven with love, warmth, and unwavering dedication. Born on October 16, 1944, in Cleveland Heights, she touched the lives of many through her kindness and compassion. The news of her passing on March 19, 2023, in Willoughby, has deeply saddened all who had the privilege of knowing her.
