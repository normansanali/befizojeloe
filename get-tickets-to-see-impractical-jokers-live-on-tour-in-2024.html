Your favorite pranksters from Staten Island are going to get goofy once again next year.

Sal, Murr and Q aka the Impractical Jokers have 11 big shows lined up at arenas, casinos, symphony halls and theatres from January through June 2024.

For those who are wondering how the oft-laugh out loud show translates to the stage, the Jokers have already figured that out.

“it’s more of a multimedia show,” Sal Vulcano told Cincinnati.com. We show videos, we tell stories. There’s some crowd stuff, interaction. It’s more geared toward our specific fans of “Jokers.” It’s more not traditional.

And if you want to see TruTv’s hidden camera comics’ ambitous show live, now is the time to snatch up your seats.

As of now, tickets are available for all shows on their 2024 calendar.

Prices start anywhere from $58 to $126 before fees on Vivid Seats for all shows at the time of publication.

Joe Gatto 2023-24 tour

On Dec. 31, 2021, former “Impractical Joker” Joe Gatto announced he was leaving the group.

In the almost two years since the announcement, he’s pivoted hard into a stand-up career and is playing venues all over the country.

“My material is made up of a lot of stories from my life growing up with the ‘Jokers’ and being friends in real life with those guys,” he told the New York Post in an exclusive interview.
