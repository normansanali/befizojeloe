Biography of Mark Stone

Mark Stone is a Canadian professional ice hockey player, currently serving as the captain and right winger for the Vegas Golden Knights of the National Hockey League (NHL). He was born on May 13, 1992, in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada.

Stone started his junior hockey career playing for the Brandon Wheat Kings of the Western Hockey League (WHL) in 2008. In his debut season, he scored 39 points in 66 games. He continued to impress throughout his junior career and eventually became team captain in the 2010-11 season. That same year, he led the league in assists and points, winning the Bob Clarke Trophy.

In the 2010 NHL Entry Draft, the Ottawa Senators picked Stone in the sixth round as the 178th overall pick. After his junior career, he joined the Senators’ American Hockey League (AHL) affiliate, the Binghamton Senators, in the 2011-12 season. His impressive performance earned him a call-up to the NHL, where he made his debut on December 23, 2012, against the Winnipeg Jets.

Stone’s rookie season in the NHL saw him score four goals and four assists in 19 games. He continued to improve over the next few seasons, finishing the 2014-15 season with 26 goals and 38 assists, earning him a nomination for the Calder Memorial Trophy as the NHL’s top rookie.

During the 2018-19 season, the Senators traded Stone to the Vegas Golden Knights. He immediately signed an eight-year contract extension worth $76 million. In his first full season with the Golden Knights, Stone scored 21 goals and 42 assists, leading the team in points and earning him the Mark Messier NHL Leadership Award.

Stone continued his success in the 2020-21 season, scoring 61 points in 55 games, ranking fourth in the NHL in assists. He also helped the Golden Knights reach the semifinals of the NHL playoffs.

Throughout his career, Stone has become known for his impressive offensive abilities, strong leadership skills, and dedication to the game. He has represented Canada in international competitions, winning a gold medal at the 2016 IIHF World Championships.

Mark Stone is a highly skilled and accomplished Canadian ice hockey player who has made a name for himself in the NHL as the captain and right winger for the Vegas Golden Knights.
