D'Andra Simmons Age 49 Wiki: Mother-Daughter Duo &amp; Husband Are Goals!

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, September 3, 2024

D'Andra Simmons is a famous Dallas socialite who inherited her business brains from her mother, Dee. She followed her parents' footsteps as she joined the family business. Then she married a military man with whom she's making other jealous! 

The Dallas-native is a Businesswoman, actress, and philanthropist, who became famous as an actress joining the cast of The Real Housewives of Dallas in its second season. 

Here are some fascinating wikis about her, ranging from her family life to her married life:

1. Get To Know D'Andra (Bio): Age, Parents

According to Wiki sites, D'Andra was born on 15 March 1969 in Dallas, Texas, to parents, Dee and Glenn R. Simmons.

D’Andra, age 49, was raised in one of the most prominent and wealthy families in the city. Her father, Glenn, alongside her uncle were self-made millionaires--which is why the Simmons name can be diversely seen throughout the city on several buildings, including the medical center as well as at Southern Methodist University.

2. D’Andra Works Alongside Her Mother, Dee, in Their Million-Dollar Company

During the 80s, D’Andra’s mother was unfortunate enough to be diagnosed with cancer.

Because cancer treatments weren’t quite paring to what they are now, she decided to take the matters in her own hands as she declined traditional therapies like chemotherapy and radiation, and went on a journey to cure her body naturally.

After that, Dee and her husband met with leading doctors, chemist, scientists and other experts on cancer and then started their own company, Ultimate Living nutritional supplements.

Fast forward to the present, D’Andra joined the company and helped her mother with the business. Since then, D’Andra has launched her beauty line from the company: Hard Night Good Morning.  

3. D’Andra Simmon’s Father Passed Away At Age 85

In March 2013, D’Andra’s father passed away at the age of 85 due to a heart attack while he was attending a board meeting in Las Vegas.

She spoke with Dallas news as she grieved about the loss of her father. She said,

“He was a wonderful Christian man. Everybody who knew my dad loved my dad.”

The memorial was held at First Baptist Church in Dallas, where he was a longtime leader.

4. Meet D’Andra Simmons’s Husband & Master Sergeant, Jeremy Lock

D’Andra Simmons is married to a retired Master Sergeant of the Air Force from the US military Jeremy Lock, who served in that post for 21 years.

The couple got married on 24 February 2014. Her husband has two children from his previous marriage whereas she has one adult son from her prior relationship.

Jeremy has been honored the Bronze Star Medal for his courage in Iraq. Furthermore, he is also a successful photojournalist who has won the Military Photographer of The Year seven times.   

Apart from his career, he’s also a devoted husband to D’Andra, who supports and encourages her in every step of the way.

5. D’Andra Simmons celebrated Four-Year Wedding Anniversary in 2018

Being a Real Housewife, D’Andra threw a glorious four-year wedding anniversary on 24 February 2018. Surrounded by family and friends, including the whole cast of the RHOD, she felt very fortunate to have a husband like Jeremy.

A day after their anniversary party, she posted a picture of herself and her husband, with a beautiful caption dedicated to not just her husband but all the people in her life.

Four years together and walking towards the future optimistically, that’s the kind of married life everyone dreams of, but only some get to live. The husband-wife duo is setting a high standard for couple goals!

6. D’Andra Simmons & ROHD costar, LeeAnne Locken are best friends!

If you’re wondering whether D’Andra Simmons’ partner in crime is merely her husband, guess again, because she has another partner in her co-star, LeeAnne Locken.

The pair shared a strong bond between each other even before D’Andra was cast on the show. They’re each other’s best friends!
