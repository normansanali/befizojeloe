How Tall Is Kanye West?

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, July 8, 2024

According to Celeb Heights, Kanye "Ye" West is 5'8", which might come as a surprise, seeing how he always takes command of each room he's in. That, and all eyes are always on him — whether it's because of the masks that he wears in public, per Page Six, or because of his feud with Peppa Pig. Either way, it's safe to say that Ye is a celebrity who always gets a lot of attention, for better or for worse.

According to his friend Tony Robbins, Ye's larger-than-life persona is mostly because of his self-confidence — or at least a renewed faith in it. Recalling the first time that he met Robbins, Ye told The New York Times in 2018, "He could look at me and you know, he could tell that I was very low. Really medicated, shoulders slumped down, and my confidence was gone, which is a lot of the root of my superpower, because if you truly have self-confidence, no one can say anything to you."

While it may seem like the father-of-four has put in a lot of work in trying to be the best version of himself, Kanye's estranged wife Kim Kardashian also credits him for bringing out the best in her for this reason, too.
