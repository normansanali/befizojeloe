Where did Josh Norman go to college and high school? Did Josh Norman play college football?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, August 10, 2024

A cornerback with the National Football League’s Carolina Panthers, Joshua Ricardo Norman was born on December 15, 1987. In the fifth round of the 2012 NFL Draft, the Panthers selected him out of Coastal Carolina’s college football program.

He has also played for the Washington Redskins, Buffalo Bills, and San Francisco 49ers. He took part in Dance with the Stars, a celebrity dancing competition show, and placed second in its 26th season in 2018.

Due to his mother and father, Norman has Native American ancestry. Alongside Kirk Cousins and Ryan Kerrigan, Norman made multiple appearances in regional auto dealer commercials while he was a member of the Redskins. As a result of the destruction caused by Hurricane Maria, which hit Puerto Rico earlier that month, Norman gave $100,000 to the recovery efforts in September 2017.

The American television program Dancing with the Stars included Norman in season 26 in 2018.

He eventually made it to the finals while skating with professional dancer Sharna Burgess, coming in second place to figure skater Adam Rippon.

During the Major League Baseball All-Star Game event week in 2018, Norman also took part in a broadcast celebrity softball game.

Where did Josh Norman go to college and high school? Did Josh Norman play college football?

He was a student at Greenwood High School. Living with his brother, Marrio Norman, who received a full football scholarship to Coastal Carolina, Norman started his collegiate career by enrolling in classes at Horry Georgetown Tech.

Norman was given the opportunity to join the Chanticleers as a walk-on the following year, in 2008. He ultimately earned a degree in communications with a dramatic arts minor.
