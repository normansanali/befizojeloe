Things are going to get better, Emiru assures fans of positive changes in OTK amidst sundry co

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, September 23, 2024

After OneTrue King (OTK) took a swift action to ask Rich Campbell for resigning the organization, members of the organization are opening up on the issue. While Asmongold was the first member to react, Emiru soon followed up to inform fans about ongoing scenario in the organization.

At the Just Chatting session of her recent livestream on Twitch, Emiru revealed that the concerned members of OTK are planning some changes on the organization, that might take a couple of months to implement. “I really wanna talk about, but I can’t yet, things are going to get better, that’s all I can say,” the streamer seemed hopeful regarding OTK’s future.

She continued to state further that she was glad after addressing situation, as it felt like a weight being lifted off from her shoulders. “If I just don’t say anything, then people are gonna ask and speculate about it forever“, Emiru observed how people might come up with their own assumptions, it’s therefore, better to hear it from her side.

Emiru refutes rumors of her leaving OTK

With so many controversies revolved around the organization, viewers were speculating that existing content creators might leave OTK. Emiru, who joined One True King last year, rubbished all these rumors as she assured her fans that she prefers to stick with the organization right now.

For her, the people who are working hard to make this organization are more important than some allegations levied on one of the members. She described OTK as “collection of people, not one person”, and if all the streamers decide to leave the organization just because of one accused person, it might become problematic for OTK.

Fans provide their opinion on OTK situation

Several fans agreed with what Emiru had to say as they believed that the Rich Campbell situation had nothing to do with the organization itself. On the flip side, fans were excited that the streamer is sticking to this organization.

While the content creator has not revealed anything about the upcoming changes in OTK, it is likely that the authorities would try to be extremely selective while choosing their content creators.
