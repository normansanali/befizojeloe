When Floyd Mayweathers daughter was jailed after stabbing altercation with rapper NBA YoungBoy

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, August 8, 2024

Former 5-division world champion Floyd Mayweather is arguably one of the richest and most successful boxers alive. He has generated billions of dollars in revenue and stayed undefeated during his professional career. Outside of his illustrious career, Mayweather has also had quite a few run-ins with the law. The same can be said about his eldest daughter, Iyanna Mayweather, who was arrested in 2020.

Floyd Mayweather’s eldest daughter was previously in a relationship with a famous rapper, NBA YoungBoy, and have a son together. However, NBA YoungBoy was also with another woman named Lapattra Jacobs who also had a kid with the rapper. The rapper and Jacobs were at his Houston home together when Iyanna Mayweather arrived. She was upset about Jacobs being at the residence, as Mayweather was still dating YoungBoy at the time. This resulted in a verbal dispute between Mayweather and Jacobs, which escalated into a physical altercation.

Iyanna then stabbed Jacobs with two knives from the kitchen and later was charged with aggravated assault. Jacobs was rushed to the nearby hospital and recounted the transpired incident. According to Iyanna, the whole alteration began due to Jacobs pulling her hair outside the house prior to the stabbing. Iyanna Mayweather’s attorney further added that they believed Mayweather was simply defending herself from being attacked.

Although Iyanna was looking at 20 years behind bars for aggravated assault with a deadly weapon, she managed to escape jail time. Iyanna pleaded guilty to the stabbing and entered a plea agreement to avoid prison. According to the agreement, she is under a special form of judge-ordered probation and has a set of rules she must abide by.

Iyanna Mayweather dragged into Floyd Mayweather and John Gotti III’s beef

The fallout from Floyd Mayweather’s recent bout with John Gotti III had many repercussions for both fighters. The fight ended up in disqualification due to John’s antics, and the referee stopped the bout. Despite the stoppage, Gotti shoved the referee aside and continued to charge at Mayweather. This led to a brawl in the ring between the camps of both fighters.

After the bout, Floyd Mayweather was put on the Italian mob’s hitlist as John Gotti III declared Mayweather his enemy for life. However, the beef did not end between the two. Gotti III’s sister Nicolette Gotti threatened Mayweather’s daughter Iyanna Mayweather. “Your daughter was ran through by a animal with 12 different baby mamas – your little circus animal – you’re all a pack of zoo animals. I swear on my kids I’m coming for your daughter, it may be 2 years, 3 years from now but I’m coming c***,” posted Nicolette on her Instagram story.

Apart from threatening Iyanna, she also dragged rapper NBA YoungBoy who was previously in a relationship with Iyanna. Given John Gotti III’s connection with the infamous Gambino crime family, his threats could be very serious. Both John Gotti III and Nicolette are grandchildren of John Gotti, who was the head of the Gambino Crime Family in the past.
