Emily Ratajkowski Steps Out in Strapless Sweater Dress and Suede Boots

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, August 4, 2024

Emily Ratajkowski would find a way to put a sultry spin on autumn street style.

The supermodel stepped out in New York City yesterday in a fit that was equal parts cozy and sexy. The centerpiece was a skintight brown sweater dress with a large bow at the top in a ribbed texture, and a flowy skirt. EmRata wore the midi with caramel-colored suede knee-high boots with stiletto heels by MVP Wardrobe and a black leather crossbody bag. For accessories, she opted for only a pair of gold hoop earrings and black oval sunglasses by Miu Miu. She wore her hair straight down and seemed to have on a glossy pink lip tint.

MEGA//Getty Images

While in Paris last month for Fashion Week, the My Body author wore another cozy fall dress—a two-tone black midi by Tory Burch. The piece had a corsetlike bustier and a breezy cotton skirt. EmRata added a subtle pop of color to the ensemble with some pointy red leather ankle boots. She also accessorized with gold hoop earrings.

And this week, she’s been rewearing a silky black midi skirt. Twice, EmRata was photographed out in a black Silk Laundry piece; on one occasion, she styled it with a leather trench coat in a burnt-orange shade and gray suede boots, and on another occasion, she wore the skirt with a cozy orange coat and bright red snakeskin boots by Alexander Wang. Ratajkowski accessorized both looks with the same gold earrings.
