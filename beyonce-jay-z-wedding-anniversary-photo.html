Tina Knowles Posts Beyonce &amp; Jay-Z Wedding Photo on Instagram

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, July 19, 2024
  • Beyoncé and Jay-Z are celebrating their twelfth wedding anniversary.
  • Beyoncé's mom, Tina Knowles, took to Instagram to share an adorable photo of the couple at their nuptials, and paid tribute to the Carters.
  • Knowles wrote, "Three gorgeous babies later and love still prevails."

Beyoncé and Jay-Z got married 12 years ago, and their family is celebrating the couple's marriage this weekend.

Beyoncé's mother, Tina Knowles, shared a sweet photo taken at the couple's wedding ceremony. The elated bride and groom can be seen celebrating having just tied the knot.

Knowles wrote, "Happy anniversary to Bey and Jay❤️❤️❤️it seemed like yesterday these guys walked down the Aisle . Those are my arms in the fluffing her dress!!! Such beautiful memories of that day now it’s twelve years later ! Three gorgeous babies later and love still prevails ❤️🙏🏾❤️❤️ ahem i designed that dress😂👏🏾👏🏾 pastor @rudyrasmus."

Beyoncé's pastor, Rudy Rasmus, re-posted the wedding picture and wrote, "It was such an honor twelve years ago to officiate and to be a part of a magical moment for an amazing couple!! A moment complete with a phenomenal dress designed by my dear friend @mstinalawson and @Beyonce was a beautiful bride. Love y’all!!!"

As pointed out by E! News, Knowles also shared the very same wedding photo on Beyoncé and Jay-Z's ninth wedding anniversary. The Carters also renewed their vows in 2018, with the superstar wearing a gorgeous Galia Lahav gown for the private affair.
