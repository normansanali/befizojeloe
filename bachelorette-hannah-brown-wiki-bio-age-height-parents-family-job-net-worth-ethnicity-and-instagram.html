Hannah Brown Biography – Hannah Brown Wiki

Hannah Brown full name Hannah Kelsey Brown, is an American model, television personality, and former beauty pageant titleholder. She was crowned Miss Alabama 2018.

Hannah Brown graduated from Tuscaloosa County High School and went on to graduate magna cum laude from the University of Alabama in 2017 with a bachelor’s degree in Communication and Information Sciences.

Hannah B become Miss Alabama USA in 2018 and used her platform to help others. She became an advocate for those suffering from depression and anxiety, something she battled with during her teenage years.

She currently works as an interior designer and stars in the 15th season of The Bachelorette.

Hannah Brown Age

Hannah Brown and Caelynn Miller-Keyes Feud

Caelynn and Hannah B competed together for the Miss USA title in 2018. They were roommates at Miss USA. According to Caelynn, Hannah “kind of snapped” when she was named First Runner-up and they had a huge falling-out.

“We’re not necessarily the best of friends,” Caelynn said. “We were roommates at Miss USA. We were super close, and then she wasn’t happy that I was first runner-up. She was mad that she didn’t place, for sure. There were a lot of hurt feelings.”

Hannah apparently has a beef with Caelyn, too. She called her ex-rommie “fake.”

“It’s just so funny because there’s not one person in the world that I have a problem with, except her,” Hannah said. “It totally scares me for Colton.”
