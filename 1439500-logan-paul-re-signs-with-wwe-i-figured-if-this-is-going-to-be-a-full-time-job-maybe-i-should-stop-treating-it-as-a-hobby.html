Logan Paul Re-Signs With WWE: I Figured If This Is Going To Be A Full-Time Job, Maybe I Should Stop

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, September 9, 2024

Logan Paul won’t be leaving WWE anytime soon.

Ahead of Saturday’s Royal Rumble premium live event, the WWE United States Champion took to Instagram to reveal that he has re-signed with the company.

“Royal Rumble this Saturday. One thing y’all didn’t know: I’ve been at PC for a couple of months. The WWE renewed my contract,” Logan Paul revealed. “Breaking news. And I figured if this is going to be a full-time job. Maybe I stop treating it as a hobby.”

Paul went on to state he believes that right now is the best time to be a WWE Superstar.

“I gotta say another thing. Right now, potentially, the best time in history to be a WWE Superstar,” Logan Paul said. “I don’t know if y’all seen the news, but WWE got partially acquired anyways Monday Night RAW by Netflix, a $5 billion deal starting 2025. We got John Cena on the podcast yesterday. The Rock joined the board at TKO. He might fire me. [laughs]”

Later in the video, Paul and his challenger for this Saturday’s Royal Rumble premium live event, Kevin Owens, exchanged words and had a pull-apart brawl to end the stream.
