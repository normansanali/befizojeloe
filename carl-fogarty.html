Biography

Carl Fogarty was born in Blackburn, England on July 1, 1965. Known as “Foggy,” he is an English Superbike racer who had one of the most successful careers the sport has ever seen, winning four World Superbike Championships. In 2014, he won the 14th series of I’m a Celebrity…Get Me Out of Here! He was a contemporary of Superbike racer Colin Edwards. He competed in his first Superbike World Championship in 1988 but did not finish.
