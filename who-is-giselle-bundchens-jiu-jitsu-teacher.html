Who is Giselle Bundchen's jiu-jitsu teacher?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, July 24, 2024

Gisele Bundchen, the renowned supermodel and philanthropist, has been in the spotlight not only for her illustrious career but also for her interest in martial arts, particularly jiu-jitsu.

Her jiu-jitsu instructor, Joaquim Valente, has been a significant figure in her life, sparking curiosity about their relationship and his background.

Introduction to Joaquim Valente

Joaquim Valente is a distinguished jiu-jitsu instructor based in Miami, Florida, and is recognized for his expertise in the martial art. He owns the Valente Brothers Jiu-Jitsu gym along with his brothers, Pedro and Giu Valente.

The gym has three locations in Florida, including Coral Gables, Pembroke Pines, and North Miami Beach.

Apart from being a martial arts instructor, Joaquim is also involved in other ventures, including 753code, which promotes a balanced life, and VB Tactical, a training facility focusing on the adaptive use of modern weaponry.

Gisele Bundchen's Relationship with Joaquim Valente

Gisele Bundchen's association with Joaquim Valente has garnered attention, particularly after her divorce from Tom Brady. The supermodel has been seen training with Joaquim and expressing her admiration for his teaching skills.

In February 2022, Gisele praised the Valente Brothers as "awesome teachers" and emphasized the importance of self-defense, especially for women, following her training sessions with them.

While speculation arose about a potential romantic relationship between Gisele and Joaquim following their vacation in Costa Rica, sources close to them clarified that they are long-time friends and that there is nothing romantic between them.

Despite the divorce, Gisele and Tom Brady have maintained an amicable co-parenting relationship for their children, Benjamin and Vivian.

Joaquim Valente's Expertise in Jiu-Jitsu

Joaquim Valente's proficiency in jiu-jitsu stems from his upbringing in a family deeply rooted in the tradition of the martial art.

Along with his brothers, Pedro and Giu, Joaquim learned jiu-jitsu from their father and began training at a very young age under the guidance of Grandmaster Helio Gracie.

Their early exposure to jiu-jitsu laid the foundation for their expertise in the art form, shaping them into accomplished instructors in the field. Joaquim's commitment to jiu-jitsu led him to establish the Valente Brothers facility in Florida, where they impart their knowledge and skills to enthusiasts and practitioners.

 

All in all, Joaquim's dedication to jiu-jitsu is further evidenced by his involvement in a notable publication.

In 2021, he, along with his brothers and Gisele Bundchen, was featured in an issue of Dust Magazine, showcasing their passion for and commitment to jiu-jitsu.

Also, he pursued a bachelor's degree in criminology at Barry University while continuing to pursue his jiu-jitsu journey, reflecting his commitment to both academics and martial arts.

The Valente Brothers Jiu-Jitsu Gym

The Valente Brothers Jiu-Jitsu gym, founded by Joaquim and his brothers, stands as a testament to their dedication to the art of jiu-jitsu.

The gym operates multiple locations in Florida, offering training and instruction to individuals seeking to learn and master the martial art.

Their approach to teaching jiu-jitsu reflects a holistic philosophy, aiming to help individuals lead balanced lives, both physically and mentally.

The legacy of the Valente family in jiu-jitsu and their commitment to sharing their expertise with the community underscores the significance of their gym in promoting the art form.

 

Joaquim Valente's impact as a jiu-jitsu instructor extends beyond his professional role; it encompasses his dedication to promoting the art form and empowering individuals through martial arts training.

His association with Gisele Bundchen has brought attention to his expertise and the Valente Brothers Jiu-Jitsu gym, highlighting the significance of martial arts in fostering physical, mental, and emotional well-being.

Through their shared commitment to jiu-jitsu, Joaquim and his brothers continue to uphold the legacy of the martial art and inspire others to embrace its principles.
