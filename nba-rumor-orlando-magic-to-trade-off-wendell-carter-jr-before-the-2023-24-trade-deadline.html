Orlando Magic to Trade-Off Wendell Carter Jr Before the 2023-24 Trade Deadline

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, July 15, 2024

The Orlando Magic are likely to move Wendell Carter in the near future. If reports are to be believed, the Orlando Magic are looking for a suitor for Carter. The Magic are living a dream this season.

When they stepped into the season, they had expected themselves to do good but with their young core, they knew they had a ceiling. But they have exploded life into the NBA. The young army is one of the most successful teams this season.

The Orlando Magic are above the likes of the Miami Heat, the New York Knicks, and the Indiana Pacers. They have a record of 21-15 this season, and they are sitting in 4th place in the Eastern Conference.

There are multiple rumors in the market involving the Orlando Magic, and most of them link them to a veteran or an experienced star. But recently, the name Wendell Carter has started to pop up in different conversations. It seems like the Orlando Magic have made up their mind about him. Here is all the information you need about Wendell Carter and his future with Orlando Magic.

Wendell Carter: Stats and Contract

The 24-year-old center and forward has played 13 games for the Orlando Magic this season. He has averaged 8.1 points, 6.4 rebounds, and 1.8 assists while shooting 40.2 percent from beyond the mark. He signed a four-year-long $50 million extension and is set to make $13.3 million this season.

Why would the Orlando Magic trade-off Wendell Carter?

The Orlando Magic’s #34 hasn’t been able to provide his service since 20th December. He has missed more than 20 games due to a fracture in his left hand. The Orlando Magic need assets to land a star, and they would fancy dumping a contract that is of less use.
