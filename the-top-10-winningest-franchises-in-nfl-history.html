The top 10 Winningest Franchises in NFL History

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

When it comes to the National Football League (NFL), there are few statistics that are as revered as the number of wins a franchise has accumulated over the years. The winningest franchises in NFL history are a true testament to the success, dedication, and talent that have graced the league since its inception. In this article, we will take a closer look at the top 10 winningest franchises in NFL history, highlighting their achievements and contributions to the sport.

1. Green Bay Packers

The Green Bay Packers hold the distinction of being the winningest franchise in NFL history. With a total of 769 wins, including regular season and playoff victories, the Packers have a storied history dating back to their founding in 1919. Led by legendary coaches such as Vince Lombardi and Curly Lambeau, the Packers have won a total of 13 NFL championships, including four Super Bowl titles.

2. Chicago Bears

The Chicago Bears come in at a close second, with an impressive 774 wins under their belt. Established in 1920, the Bears have been a dominant force in the league, showcasing their prowess on both offense and defense. Led by iconic figures such as Walter Payton and Mike Ditka, the Bears have won nine NFL championships, including one Super Bowl victory.

3. New York Giants

The New York Giants have long been a cornerstone of the NFL, boasting a total of 722 wins. Founded in 1925, the Giants have consistently been a competitive franchise, known for their strong defense and talented roster. With four Super Bowl titles and eight NFL championships, the Giants have left an indelible mark on the league.

4. Pittsburgh Steelers

The Pittsburgh Steelers have established themselves as one of the most successful franchises in NFL history. With 749 wins, the Steelers have been a force to be reckoned with since their inception in 1933. Led by legendary coaches such as Chuck Noll and Bill Cowher, the Steelers have won six Super Bowls and have a total of eight AFC championships to their name.

5. New England Patriots

The New England Patriots have enjoyed an incredible run of success in recent decades, accumulating a total of 768 wins. Since their establishment in 1959, the Patriots have been synonymous with excellence, thanks in large part to the leadership of head coach Bill Belichick and the skills of quarterback Tom Brady. With six Super Bowl titles and 11 AFC championships, the Patriots have solidified their place among the winningest franchises in NFL history.

6. Miami Dolphins

The Miami Dolphins have a rich history in the NFL, with 526 wins to their name. Established in 1966, the Dolphins experienced a period of dominance in the 1970s, led by the legendary coach Don Shula. The team's undefeated season in 1972 remains one of the greatest accomplishments in NFL history, and the Dolphins have also won two Super Bowls in their storied past.

7. San Francisco 49ers

The San Francisco 49ers have long been considered one of the most successful franchises in NFL history. With 623 wins, the 49ers have been a perennial contender since their establishment in 1946. Coached by icons such as Bill Walsh and led by legendary quarterbacks like Joe Montana and Steve Young, the 49ers have won five Super Bowl titles and have a total of six NFC championships.

8. Dallas Cowboys

The Dallas Cowboys have amassed a total of 526 wins since their inception in 1960. Known as "America's Team," the Cowboys have been a consistent presence in the playoffs and have enjoyed a storied history. Under the leadership of head coach Tom Landry and the talents of players like Troy Aikman and Emmitt Smith, the Cowboys have won five Super Bowl titles and have a total of eight NFC championships.

9. Oakland/Los Angeles/Las Vegas Raiders

The Raiders franchise has had a nomadic history, moving from Oakland to Los Angeles and now to Las Vegas. With 473 wins, the Raiders have been a successful franchise since their establishment in 1960. Known for their fierce and aggressive playstyle, the Raiders have won three Super Bowl titles and have a total of four AFC championships.

10. Washington Football Team

Rounding out the top 10 winningest franchises in NFL history is the Washington Football Team, formerly known as the Washington Redskins. With 602 wins, the franchise has a storied past that includes three Super Bowl titles and five NFC championships. Established in 1932, the team has seen its fair share of ups and downs but remains a respected and competitive franchise.

The top 10 winningest franchises in NFL history have left an indelible mark on the league. From the Green Bay Packers' unparalleled success to the Washington Football Team's storied past, these franchises have showcased their talent, determination, and dedication to the sport. As the NFL continues to evolve, it will be exciting to see which teams rise to the top and join the ranks of these legendary franchises.
