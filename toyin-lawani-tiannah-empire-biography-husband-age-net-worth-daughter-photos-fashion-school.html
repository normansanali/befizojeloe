Toyin Lawani (Tiannah Empire) Biography: Husband, Age, Net Worth, Daughter, Photos, Fashion School

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, July 20, 2024

Biography

Toyin Lawani who became popular with Tiannah Place Empire is the Founder and CEO of top fashion brand, Tiannah’s Empire.

Toyin Lawani (Tiannah Empire) (born 1 March 1982) is a 38 years old Nigerian celebrity fashion stylist, fashion entrepreneur and an interior decorator.

Early Life

Toyin Lawani (Tiannah) was born in 1982 to an illustrious Ekiti family.

Her father, Olanrewaju Lawani was a politician and he worked with General Yakubu Gowon. While her mother was a successful business woman who ran a textile business in the US.

Her grandfather was a Commissioner of Police while her grandmother is the Regent of Ikole Ikiti, Ekiti State.

Education

Whilst her father wanted her to be a lawyer, her mother groomed her to be an entrepreneur.

She studied English Language at the prestigious University of Lagos.

She attended the Oprah Benson Training Institute. She was also educated at the Los Angeles Fashion School and obtained a diploma in fashion.

She has also enrolled for training courses at Johnyim Fashion and Beauty Institute, South Korea and Exodus Hair School, India.

Career

While studying at the university, she opened a mini supermarket, boutique and a spa. She achieved this feat at a young age of sixteen.

Over the years, she has grown to be one of the leading stylists in the country. She has designed outfit for many celebrities across the world such as Banky W, Funke Akindele, Wizkid, Tiwa Savage, D’Prince, Keri Hilson, Ciara, Natalie Nunn, Emma Nyra, amongst others.

She is the founder and creative director of Tiannah’s Place Empire. She also runs a fashion academy – Tiannah Styling Fashion Academy.

She also own a beauty products line called Tiannah’s Glow.

Toyin is the brain behind Denim Agbada designs and the collections– Dream Collection, The Transformer Collection, Face Collection, Asiko Collection, Ready To Wear Collection and many others.

She has also won a couple of awards such as the City People Entertainment Special Recognition Award and Stylist Of The Year Award (2015).

She was a guest judge of the show, Next Top Designer show held in Lagos.

She is the creative director of Exquisite Magazine and her designs have been featured in different top magazines.

She was credited as the first Nigerian fashion designer to organize a celebrity and kids runway show simultaneously.

She is also a social media personality with a large followership on Instagram.

Her official Instagram handle is @tiannahsplaceempire. She has over 1.3M followers on the platform.

Personal Life

Toyin Lawani (Tiannah Empire) was formerly engaged to upcoming music artiste, Lord Trigg. They have a child together. It is a boy named Lord Maine.

She has a daughter from a previous relationship.

She was rumoured to have dated a former Lagos State Commissioner for Infrastructure, Segun Oniru.

She was also alleged to have been in a relationship with Taiwo Afolabi, who is the MD/CEO of Sifax Group. Taiwo Afolabi is the father of singer, L.A.X.

Net Worth

The beautiful designer, Toyin Lawani (Tiannah Empire) has an estimated net worth of $5,000,000.

She reportedly bought a house worth 300 million naira in Lekki.
