Jennifer Lawrence, Amy Schumer Prove Theyre BFFs at the Golden Globes 2016

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, September 21, 2024

Forget squad goals, it’s all about best friend goals when it comes to Amy Schumer and Jennifer Lawrence. The two BFFs presented at the 2016 Golden Globes on Sunday, January 10, and had the audience laughing.

“They’re best friends by the way, they want me to tell you that,” host Ricky Gervais said while introducing Lawrence, 25, and Schumer, 34, before they presented clips for their movies which are both nominees for Motion Picture Comedy.

“Hello, I’m J.Law … and I’m A. Schu,” the stars told the crowd. 

“You can’t just give yourself a celeb nickname,” Lawrence informed her friend, cracking up the audience.

The BFFs jokingly called off their friendship after they were both nominated in the same category, Best Actress in a Comedy. “No. The friendship is completely over,” Schumer joked to Entertainment Tonight. “I just blocked her number. It’s over.”

The two became friends after Lawrence reached out to Schumer to praise her comedy Trainwreck, and they’re currently working on a script for an untitled project together, in which they will both star.
