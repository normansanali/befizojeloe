Dove Cameron Height, Age, Boyfriend, Biography, Family &amp; Net worth

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, August 25, 2024

Dove Cameron (born January 15, 1996) is an American Actress and Singer from Seattle, Washington. She is famous for her dual lead role as Liv/Maddie Rooney in the Disney Channel show “Liv and Maddie” from 2013 to 2017. Furthermore, the 22-year-old actress also portrayed a role of Kayla Morgan in the movie “Cloud 9” (the 2014 Disney Channel original film).

Her real full birth name is Chloe Celeste Hosterman. Moreover, the actress’ role as Mal in Descendants (2015), the sequel Descendants 2 (2017) gained a lot of popularity. Cameron will also play the same role in the third installment of the film releasing in 2019.

In addition, Dove also has a recurring role as Ruby in the television series “Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.” (2018). The actress also won “2018 Daytime Emmy Awards” for Liv and Maddie show. Chloe received the award in the category of Outstanding Performer in a Children’s Series.

As a singer, she sang the song “On Top of the World” by Imagine Dragons on her show Liv and Maddie. Her cover peaked on the Billboard Kid Digital Songs chart at the 17th position.

Profile, Parents & Early Life

The actress was born on 15th January 1996 in Seattle, Washington, United States. As of 2018, Dove Cameron’s age is 22 years old. Her father “Philip Alan Hosterman” was a jewelry importer.

Furthermore, the actress’ mother Bonnie Wallace divorced her husband when Dove was young. The actress also has an older sister named Claire Hosterman. Dove attended Sakai Intermediate School. Later, she graduated from Burbank High School.

Moreover, in 2011, Cameron’s dad died when she was 15 years old. Later, the actress changed her legal name in honor of her father who nicknamed her Dove.

Dating History, Spouse & Boyfriend

Dove Cameron started her relationship with her boyfriend Ryan McCartan (actor and singer) in 2013. They first met on the sets of Liv and Maddie where Ryan played Diggie on the show.

The couple also announced about their engagement in April 2016. However, they broke up later in October 2016. Currently, the actress is dating Thomas Doherty (Scottish Actor). Dove and her partner met on the set of “Descendants 2”. They made their relationship public in February 2017.
