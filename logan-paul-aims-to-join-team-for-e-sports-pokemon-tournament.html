Logan Paul Aims to Join Team for E-Sports Pokmon Tournament

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, September 8, 2024

Logan Paul has not only made his mark as a top-notch social media influencer, but a remarkable WWE superstar as well. Apart from that, he has had an avid interest in gaming and is looking to join a team that is addicted to Pokémon like him.

Paul took to his X to reveal that he had become obsessed with the video game ‘Pokémon Unite for an entire year. Now, he and his friend Nick wanted to take part in the game’s Championship tournament.

Logan stated that they did not have a team and were looking to join one or would like to make their own faction to compete in the E-sports championship, while also talking about their prowess in the game.

“One year ago, @NickKyrgios came on my podcast and told me about a Nintendo Switch game called “Pokemon Unite.” Given my obsession with Pokemon, I finally started playing & now I’m addicted. Nick & I want to compete in the Championship Tournament but we don’t have a team… anyone looking for two strong teammates OR want to form an eSports team with us? We’re both Master Class, quick decisions makers, 80% win rate & high scorers. Tag us on X, Instagram, or e-mail us here: JoinPokemonUniteTeam@gmail.com.”

On the WWE side, Logan Paul is the current WWE United States champion, winning the title from WWE Hall of Famer, Rey Mysterio at WWE Crown Jewel. He recently made a successful title defense against Kevin Owens at the Royal Rumble event.

With WrestleMania 40 coming up, it would be interesting to see who steps up to The Maverick for the US title for the Show of Shows.
