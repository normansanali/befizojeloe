Who Is Christina Garibaldi? Everything To Know

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, July 13, 2024

Learn about Christina Garibaldi Wikipedia and much more in this brief article about the wonderful journalist.

Christina Garibaldi worked on Britney Spear’s documentary titled Britney Spears: I Am Femme Fatale in 2011and interviewed the pop star in 2013. She co-hosted the MTV Movie Awards in 2014 alongside Zendaya, Tyler Posey, and Josh Horowitz.

Is Garibaldi Active On Social Media?

The TV Reporter is quite active on social media platforms including Twitter and Instagram.

She can be found on Twitter under the username @XtinaGaribaldi. 7.3k people follow her on the platform.

Similarly, her Instagram handle is @christina_garibaldi. She has a following of 10.2k on IG.

Who Is Christina Husband?

Christina Garibaldi’s husband is Michael LaMastro.

When asked by her college if she keeps in touch with the ex-students, Christina answered that she still talked to some of her roommates. She added, she got married to one of the alumni named Michael LaMastro.

She has a daughter named Gianna. The journalist is currently pregnant and is expecting a baby boy in June of 2021.

Similarly, Christina Garibaldi has a sister named Vanessa Garibaldi.

Christina Garibaldi Age: How Old Is The Reporter?

Christina actual age is currently unknown.

However, it is speculated that she is currently in her late 30s to early 40s.

Details regarding her date of birth are unavailable too.

Does Christina Garibaldi Have A Bio On Wikipedia?

Unfortunately, Garibaldi doesn’t have a Wikipedia page dedicated to her.

Hence, details regarding her are scarce on the internet.

Christina graduated from Marist College in 2006. The college is located in Poughkeepsie, NY.

Likewise, She studied at the School of Communication and was a member of Marist College Television. This education helped her prepare for her big job with MTV as a TV reporter. She started off as an intern at MTV.
