Get To Know Arielle Kebbel Married, Boyfriend, Relationship Status

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, October 1, 2024

May 2, 2022 ep of 9-1-1 saw Arielle Kebbel as “Lucy” and many were surprised to see the actress making her debut in the popular American procedural drama. So, naturally, stepping into the limelight again, Arielle once again had fans intrigued all about her; especially her love life.

Thus, here we are with answers to your queries — is Arielle Kebbel married or is she still enjoying the dating game?

Is Arielle Kebbel Married?

No, Arielle Kebbel isn’t married as of 2022. The actress is still busy building her career and isn’t ready to take a husband just yet.

Born Feb 19, 1985, Arielle started her acting career in the early 2000s and quickly emerged as one of Hollywood’s most sought-after talents. Over the years, she has starred in several films and TV shows — multiple of which made have their debuts at prestigious film festivals.

Some of her popular works include — American Pie Presents: Band Camp (2005), The Grudge 2 (2006), Vampires Suck (2010), Life Unexpected (2010), 90210 (2011 – 2013), The Vampire Diaries (2009 – 2017), Midnight, Texas (2017 – 2018), and Lincoln Rhyme: Hunt for the Bone Collector (2020).

The last we saw her she was busy filming 9-1-1. Her character, Lucy on the show was an LAFD Firefighter who worked at rival Firehouse 147. Soon after watching Lucy’s interaction with other cast members on 9-1-1, many viewers took it to their Twitter to express how much they wanted Arielle to become a regular cast member.

And seeing her building romance with Buck, it did seem like she will be sticking around for quite a while

Does Arielle Kebbel Have A Boyfriend?

Yes, Arielle Kebbel has had quite a few boyfriends. Here’s a list of them according to the timeline.

2004: Brahman Turner — Arielle dated the Daydreamer actor for 2 long years.

2010: Kevin Connolly — Though Arielle and Kevin never confessed their relationship, the two were spotted leaving an event together. So, it wasn’t long before fans started digging around.

2012: Aaron Bay-Schuck — Arielle and Aaron (a businessperson and songwriter) started their relationship at the beginning of 2012. Since then, the two stars started appearing on red carpets for several years. In Sept 2012, Arielle even revealed that her boyfriend then was appointed as the President and A&R of Interscope Geffen A&M Records.

At the time of writing this article, Aaron was married to Hannah (Montgomery) Bay-Schuck for almost 2 years.

2018: Nic Roldan — The actress and the model/American Polo player Nic started appearing in photos on Arielle’s socials in late 2018, and soon fans knew that the two were together. There were also reports that the two spent their 2019 Halloween weekend together at the Standard Spa in Miami Beach.

If you don’t know much about Nic, he was once the highest-rated American Polo Player with an 8-goal handicap rating. Also, he was the youngest polo player in the world to win the prestigious U.S. Open Polo title at the age of 15.

As of 2022, Nic served as the president of R Polo Holdings and worked as a real estate associate at Equestrian Sotheby’s International Realty. Also, he was dating Tom Selleck’s daughter, Hannah Selleck at the time. Similar to her boyfriend, Hannah is an accomplished horse rider.

2020: Anonymous — Arielle started dating her mystery boyfriend in March of 2020. According to the actress, they first met in New York while she was there filming for six months. Her secret boyfriend is from Brooklyn and he often graced Arielle’s IG. However, the posts now have been deleted — hinting the two might have broken up.

Arielle Kebbel Relationship Status

Arielle Kebbel’s relationship status (as of 2022) is difficult to figure out. Since the actress always cherished privacy in her dating life, but at the same time didn’t hesitate to flaunt pictures with her male companions, it made her love life even more ambiguous.

The last we checked, Arielle had removed all hints about her previous boyfriends from her IG. However, she did leave many selfies of her next to past male co-stars like Dwayne Johnson and Jamie Dornan.
